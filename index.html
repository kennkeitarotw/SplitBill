📖 Help & FAQ
1

Add participants

Type each person's name in Step 1 and optionally add their bank account number for settlement. Click Add or press Enter.

2

Enter expenses

In Step 2, enter the expense name, amount, and who paid. Choose which people to split it among, then click Add expense.

Use the quick-fill buttons (Dinner, Taxi…) to pre-fill the category and name instantly.

3

Scan a receipt (OCR)

Tap 📷 Scan Receipt in the header. Upload or photograph a receipt — items are detected automatically. Tap each person's avatar on a line to assign them. Adjust tax/discount if needed, then import as one grouped expense.

4

Add session tax or tips

Use the 💰 Session Tax & Tips bar to apply a percentage tip or flat service charge to the whole bill. It adjusts settlement amounts proportionally without modifying individual expenses.

5

Save sessions (multiple trips)

Click Save in the session bar to store the current trip under its title. Later, select it from the dropdown and click Load to restore it. You can save multiple trips (e.g. Taipei Trip, Paris Trip).

6

Settle up & export

The Settle up panel shows the minimum payments needed. Click ⬇️ Export to download a JSON backup, a text summary, or copy it to clipboard to share in chat.

How do I switch currency? ▾
Use the Currency dropdown in the session bar. All amounts display in the selected currency symbol. The exchange rate is not applied — amounts are entered directly in the chosen currency.
Can one expense be paid by multiple people? ▾
Yes. After an expense is created, click ✏️ Edit on the expense card. In the "Paid by" section, enter how much each person contributed. The amounts must sum to the expense total.
How does the OCR receipt scan work? ▾
It uses Tesseract.js to run OCR entirely in your browser — no data is sent to any server. For best results use a flat, well-lit photo with clear text. After scanning, review and correct items before importing.
What is "Equal vs Custom vs Percentage" split? ▾
Equal divides the amount evenly. Custom lets you enter a relative weight per person (e.g. 2:1:1). Percentage lets you specify exact percentages that should sum to 100%.
Is my data saved? ▾
The active session auto-saves to your browser's localStorage on every change. Use Save in the session bar to create named snapshots, and ⬇️ Export → JSON for a file backup you can restore later.
How do I restore a JSON backup? ▾
Click ⬇️ Export → Import JSON and select the file. Your people, expenses and session adjustments will be restored.
How does the session tax/tip work? ▾
Session adjustments scale all settlement amounts by a factor without modifying individual expense records. For example, a 10% service charge multiplies every person's net amount by 1.1. You can add multiple adjustments that stack together.
